What This Is

SORA acts as a local communication layer for brands that already have interest from Japan but no Japanese-facing operation. We do not replace your main brand team. We make sure Japanese users can understand, engage, and ask questions without being left unanswered.

Who This Is For

This works best for brands that already have Japanese followers, engagement, or inquiries — and currently have no Japanese captions, replies, or support flow. Whether you already ship to Japan or are still exploring the market, if Japanese users are finding you, we can help.

How It Works
01
Account Setup
You create and own the Japanese Instagram account. We receive admin access and operate it on your behalf.
02
Content Handoff
You share upcoming creative assets and original English captions through a shared folder, preferably Google Drive.
03
Japanese Localization
We rewrite the post for Japanese users. This is not direct translation; it is localization adjusted to tone and context.
04
Approval & Posting
You review the draft when needed. Once approved, we publish to the Japanese account. Time-sensitive launches can be posted simultaneously with your main account.
05
Japanese User Support
We respond to Japanese comments, DMs, and basic website inquiries. More sensitive cases are escalated to your team before reply.
06
Monthly Reporting
We share a simple monthly summary covering post output, follower movement, engagement, and notable customer questions.

Why Brands Use This

Most brands do not need a full Japan launch at the beginning. They need a lightweight way to stop losing people who are already interested. SORA exists for that gap: a low-friction way to maintain communication with Japanese users before building a larger local operation.
